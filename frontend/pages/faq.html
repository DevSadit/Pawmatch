Everything you need to know about PawMatch

General

PawMatch is a comprehensive pet platform for Bangladesh where you can adopt pets, trade or rehome pets, connect with a community of pet lovers, use our Match Finder quiz, and discover pets using PawSwipe — our Tinder-style pet swiping experience.

Adoption

Browse or PawSwipe to find a pet you like. Click "View Profile" to see full details. If interested, click "Adopt Me" to see adoption steps and contact the shelter or owner directly. Each listing shows the owner's contact information.

Absolutely! Create an account, go to "Add Pet," and set the listing type to "Rehome." Your pet's profile will be visible to potential adopters. You can edit or remove the listing at any time from "Manage Listings."
PawSwipe

PawSwipe is our Tinder-inspired pet discovery feature. Instead of browsing a list, you see one pet at a time and swipe right to like (save) or swipe left to skip. On desktop, you can click the ♥ or ✕ buttons. Liked pets are saved to "My Matches."

All liked pets are saved to your browser's local storage and appear in "My Matches." You can access this from the navbar (under your account menu) or directly at /pages/my-matches.html.
Safety

If you spot a suspicious or misleading listing, please use the Contact page to report it. Include the pet's name and listing ID. Our team reviews reports and follows up as quickly as possible.

Listings are community-submitted, so we recommend meeting in a public place, asking questions directly, and verifying the pet's health with a vet before completing any transaction.
