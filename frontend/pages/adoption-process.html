How to Adopt a Pet

Follow these simple steps to give a pet a forever home

1
Browse or Swipe

Use Browse Pets or PawSwipe to explore available animals. Filter by type, location, and status. Save the ones you love to My Matches.

2
View Pet Profile

Click on any pet to see their full profile: age, breed, personality, vaccination status, and compatibility details.

3
Express Interest

Click "Adopt Me" on the pet's profile page to see contact details for the shelter or owner. Reach out to express your interest.

4
Meet the Pet

Arrange a meet-and-greet with the pet before committing. Bring family members to ensure everyone is comfortable with the new addition.

5
Complete Paperwork

Sign the adoption agreement provided by the shelter or owner. Some shelters require a home assessment for certain breeds.

6
Welcome Home!

Bring your new family member home. Give them time to adjust — the first few days may be quiet. Within weeks, they'll blossom!

📋 Adoption Requirements
  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Must have permission from landlord if renting
  • Must have appropriate housing for the pet's needs
  • Must agree to regular vet check-ups
  • Must not have a history of animal abuse
  • Must provide emergency contact information
✅ Our Adoption Promise
  • All listed pets have been health-checked
  • Vaccination status is clearly displayed
  • Behavioral history is shared when available
  • We support adopters with post-adoption guidance
  • Re-homing support if circumstances change
  • Community forum for questions and advice
